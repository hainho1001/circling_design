Creating an account is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Click "Get Started" on our homepage
  2. Choose your role: Cargo Owner or Fleet Operator
  3. Fill in your business details and contact information
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile setup

Once verified, you'll have immediate access to our platform and can start shipping or bidding on jobs right away.

To register as a fleet operator, you'll need:

  • Valid commercial vehicle registration documents
  • Insurance certificates (minimum coverage requirements apply)
  • Driver licenses and qualifications
  • Business registration or operating license
  • Tax identification numbers

Our verification team reviews all documents within 24-48 hours to ensure compliance with European transport regulations.

Our pricing is transparent and competitive:

  • For Cargo Owners: Pay a small commission (3-5%) only on successful shipments
  • For Fleet Operators: No monthly fees - we deduct a service fee (2-3%) from completed jobs
  • Platform Access: Free to join and browse opportunities
  • Premium Features: Advanced analytics and priority support available

No hidden fees, no setup costs, and no minimum commitments.

Payment processing is fast and secure:

  • Fleet Operators: Payments are released within 24 hours of delivery confirmation
  • Payment Methods: Bank transfer, PayPal, or digital wallets
  • Currency: Payments in EUR, GBP, or local currencies
  • Invoicing: Automatic invoice generation and tax compliance

All transactions are protected by our secure payment system with fraud protection.

Our advanced tracking system provides complete visibility:

  • GPS Integration: Real-time location updates every 30 seconds
  • Route Monitoring: Planned vs. actual route comparison
  • ETA Updates: Dynamic delivery time estimates
  • Alerts: Automatic notifications for delays or route deviations
  • Mobile App: Track shipments on iOS and Android devices

Both cargo owners and fleet operators have access to the same tracking information for complete transparency.

Our intelligent bidding system ensures fair pricing:

  1. Job Posting: Cargo owners post shipment details and requirements
  2. Smart Matching: AI suggests relevant jobs to qualified fleet operators
  3. Competitive Bidding: Fleet operators submit competitive bids
  4. Selection Process: Cargo owners review bids, ratings, and fleet credentials
  5. Award & Execute: Job awarded to best bid, contract automatically created

The system promotes fair competition while ensuring quality service delivery.

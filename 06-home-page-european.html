How It Works

Get started in minutes with our simple and efficient process

1

Create Your Account

Sign up as a shipper or fleet owner. Complete your profile and get verified to start using the platform immediately.

2

Post or Find Loads

Shippers post their freight needs, while carriers browse available loads and submit competitive bids.
